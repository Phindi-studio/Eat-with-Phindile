Creamy Garlic Butter Mussels

Recipe by: Nonkululeko Mazibuko

Serves: 2-3

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Bring a taste of the coast to your kitchen with this simple, creamy mussel recipe. It’s rich, aromatic, and perfect for seafood lovers who enjoy quick, flavorful meals.

Ingredients

  • 1 white onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp crushed garlic (or more to taste)
  • 1 tsp fish spice
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp mixed herbs
  • 500g mussels (cleaned and debearded)
  • ½ cup fresh cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter in a large pot over low heat.
  2. Add both onions and sauté until soft and fragrant.
  3. Stir in the garlic, fish spice, turmeric, and mixed herbs. Cook for about a minute to release the aromas.
  4. Add the mussels and toss to coat them in the spice mixture.
  5. Pour in the fresh cream, stir gently, then cover the pot.
  6. Cook on low heat for 10 minutes, or until all mussel shells open. Discard any that don’t.
  7. Stir once more to mix the sauce evenly and serve hot with crusty bread or rice.

Tip: Add a squeeze of lemon and a sprinkle of fresh parsley for extra freshness.
