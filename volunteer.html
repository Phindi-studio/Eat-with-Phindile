Become a Volunteer Writer for Eat with Phindile

Are you a graduate who wants writing experience you can actually use on a CV or portfolio? Eat With Phindile is inviting volunteer contributors to write recipes, food articles, and simple cooking content for the blog.

This is a peer collaboration, not a job. I’m also a graduate building experience, so we grow together.

What You’ll Do

You can choose one or more writing styles:

  • Recipe posts – from your own kitchen, family meals, or budget-friendly ideas
  • Short food articles – food tips, kitchen basics, food hacks
  • Personal food stories – how you learned to cook, family food memories, etc.

No formal writing or journalism background is required.

Every published piece will include your full byline (your name + optional short bio + social media handle).

What You’ll Get

  • Your name and social media handle featured on every post you write
  • A permanent live link you can use on your CV, portfolio, or LinkedIn
  • Written feedback to help you improve each piece before it goes live
  • Experience writing for a real blog, not just academic assignments

Important

This is an unpaid volunteer opportunity.

It is best suited for graduates who want:

  • Published writing samples
  • Something credible to list under “Experience” on a CV
  • A place to practice writing publicly with visible credit

If you are looking for paid writing work, please do not apply.

Time Commitment

  • Flexible and self-paced
  • One piece at a time, with agreed deadlines
  • No required weekly hours unless you request ongoing work

How to Join

Step 1: Head over to our Contact Page to send your application.

Include in your message:

  • Your name
  • What kind of content you’d like to write

You’ll receive a reply with next steps and a writing brief.
